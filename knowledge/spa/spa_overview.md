# Six Senses Spa — Overview

## Property
Porto Elounda, Elounda Mare, Elounda Peninsula

## Category
spa

## Overview
Six Senses Spa at Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort offers a holistic wellness experience for guests of the Elounda Collection properties.

The spa combines high-touch service, wellness expertise, treatments, thalassotherapy, thermal experiences, beauty services, personalized wellness programs and relaxation therapies.

Guests from Porto Elounda, Elounda Mare and Elounda Peninsula may use the spa services, subject to availability and advance reservation.

## Opening Hours
Daily: 09:00 – 19:00

## Reservations
Advance reservation is required.

For reservations, guests may contact the Six Senses Spa directly.

## Contact
Internal Extension: 6100

## Main Spa Experiences
The spa offers:

- Massages
- Facials
- Body treatments
- Ayurveda aromatherapy treatments
- Wellness programs
- Yoga and meditation experiences
- Detox programs
- Sleep programs
- Fitness programs
- Thalassotherapy and thermal suite experiences
- Junior spa treatments
- Hair salon and beauty services

## Spa Facilities
Available facilities include:

- Indoor and outdoor thalassotherapy pool
- Hydrotherapy experiences
- Roman steam bath
- Caldarium
- Saunarium
- Finnish sauna
- Thermal suite
- Relaxation areas

## Booking Notes
Guests are encouraged to book treatments in advance, especially during high season.

Some wellness programs are available as multi-day experiences and may require prior consultation.

For specific treatments, prices, availability or personalized recommendations, guests should contact the spa team at extension 6100.

## Important Concierge Guidance
If a guest asks for a spa treatment recommendation, Estia should suggest contacting the spa team for availability and personalization.

If a guest asks for exact prices of a treatment not listed in the current knowledge base, Estia should say that pricing depends on the selected treatment and recommend contacting the spa at extension 6100.
