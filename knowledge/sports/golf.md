# Golf Experience

## Property

Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort

## Category

service

## Service Type

Golf

## Overview

Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort offers one of the most unique golf experiences in Greece.

The resort features a dedicated golf facility where guests can enjoy golf practice, lessons and recreational play while overlooking the beautiful landscape of Elounda and Mirabello Bay.

Golf forms an important part of the resort's sports and leisure offering and is suitable for both beginners and experienced players.

## Golf Facilities

Available facilities may include:

* Golf Course
* Golf Academy
* Practice Areas
* Putting Areas
* Golf Lessons
* Equipment Rental
* Private Coaching Sessions

## Suitable For

The golf experience is suitable for:

* Beginners
* Intermediate golfers
* Experienced golfers
* Families
* Corporate guests
* Individual lessons
* Group lessons

## Lessons

Golf lessons may be available through professional instructors.

Lesson availability may vary depending on season and instructor schedules.

## Equipment

Golf equipment and practice equipment may be available through the golf facility.

Availability should be confirmed through Concierge or the Golf Academy.

## Reservations

Advance reservations are recommended for:

* Lessons
* Coaching sessions
* Tee times
* Golf programs

## Recommended For

The golf experience is recommended for:

* Sports enthusiasts
* Families
* Beginners wishing to learn golf
* Experienced golfers seeking practice
* Guests looking for outdoor activities

## Location

Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort

## Concierge Notes

Golf is one of the signature sports experiences of Porto Elounda.

Guests interested in lessons, equipment rental or tee times should be directed to the Golf Academy or Concierge.

## Important Guidance for Estia

When guests ask whether golf is available, answer that Porto Elounda offers golf facilities and golf experiences.

When guests ask for golf lessons, explain that lessons may be available through professional instructors and recommend contacting Concierge.

When guests ask about equipment rental, explain that availability should be confirmed with the golf facility.

When guests ask how to book golf activities, recommend contacting Concierge for current schedules and availability.
