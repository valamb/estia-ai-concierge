# Elies Beach House

## Property

## Category

restaurant

## Cuisine

Contemporary International Cuisine with Mediterranean influences.

## Overview

Elies Beach House is nestled beneath the tranquil canopy of an ancient olive grove, offering a serene seaside dining experience where nature and elegance exist in perfect harmony.

The restaurant's culinary concept celebrates contemporary international cuisine while embracing the relaxed sophistication of the Mediterranean lifestyle.

Each dish is thoughtfully crafted to evoke a sense of place while inviting guests on a journey of flavor and discovery.

Set against a timeless natural backdrop, Elies Beach House creates a modern dining experience defined by effortless luxury, warm hospitality and the gentle rhythm of the sea.
