## Overview

Start your day with an exquisite breakfast at Calypso Restaurant, enjoying panoramic views over Mirabello Bay.

The restaurant offers a carefully curated à la carte breakfast experience combining local Cretan delicacies with international favorites.

Calypso provides a refined and tranquil morning atmosphere, perfectly suited to the exclusive character of Elounda Peninsula.
