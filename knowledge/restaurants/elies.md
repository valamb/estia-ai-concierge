## Overview

Elies Restaurant offers a relaxed Mediterranean dining experience within Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort.

Inspired by the flavors, ingredients and traditions of the Mediterranean, the restaurant combines fresh seasonal products with elegant presentation and warm hospitality.

The atmosphere is refined yet relaxed, making it suitable for both couples and families seeking quality dining in a comfortable environment.
