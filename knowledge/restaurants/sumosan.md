## Overview

Sumosan is an internationally acclaimed contemporary Japanese restaurant that has established a presence in some of the world's most vibrant cities.

At Elounda Peninsula, Sumosan offers a sophisticated waterfront dining experience overlooking the Aegean Sea, combining exceptional cuisine, elegant service and a stylish atmosphere.

Renowned for its modern interpretation of Japanese cuisine, Sumosan has become a destination for food enthusiasts, celebrities and guests seeking an elevated dining experience.
