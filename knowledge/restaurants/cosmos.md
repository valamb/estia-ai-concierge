# Cosmos Restaurant

## Property

Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort

## Category

restaurant

## Restaurant Type

International Buffet Restaurant

## Cuisine

International cuisine and local Cretan specialties.

## Overview

Cosmos International Buffet Restaurant invites guests on a culinary journey through flavors from around the world.

The restaurant combines international specialties with local Cretan dishes, offering freshness, variety and a relaxed luxury dining experience.

The kitchen becomes a stage where aromas, colors and dishes are prepared before guests' eyes, creating an engaging dining experience for guests of all ages.

## Service Style

Buffet restaurant with live cooking stations.
