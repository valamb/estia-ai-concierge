## Overview

Welcome to the Yacht Club Restaurant, where culinary excellence meets breathtaking sea views.

Perched dramatically above the beach, the restaurant offers one of the most picturesque dining locations within the Elounda Collection.

The menu celebrates authentic Greek cuisine, fresh local seafood and traditional Mediterranean flavors, prepared with the highest quality ingredients.

## Concierge Notes

Yacht Club is one of the signature restaurants of Elounda Mare.

Guests looking for seafood and authentic Greek cuisine should normally be directed to Yacht Club Restaurant.

Advance reservations are recommended, especially during high season and for Fisher's Evening.

## Important Guidance for Estia

When guests ask for seafood recommendations, Yacht Club Restaurant should normally be suggested.

When guests ask for authentic Greek cuisine, Yacht Club Restaurant should be included among the primary recommendations.

When guests ask about Fisher's Evening, explain that it showcases the freshest local seafood and catch of the day.

When guests ask for a restaurant overlooking the sea, Yacht Club should be recommended.
