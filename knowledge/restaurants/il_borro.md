## Overview

Il Borro Tuscan Bistro offers a unique dining experience inspired by the renowned Il Borro estate in Tuscany, owned by the Ferragamo family.

The restaurant embraces the philosophy of organic farming, sustainability and authentic Italian hospitality.

Situated along the waterfront of Elounda Peninsula, Il Borro combines traditional Italian recipes with modern culinary techniques, creating an elegant dining experience in a spectacular seaside setting.

## Signature Concept

The restaurant reflects the culture and culinary traditions of the Tuscan estate of Il Borro, celebrating:

* Organic ingredients

* Authentic Italian cuisine
* Premium wines
* Seasonal products
