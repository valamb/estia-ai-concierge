## Overview

Nestled amidst lush gardens by the waterside, Old Mill Restaurant offers one of the most celebrated dining experiences within the Elounda Collection.

Centered around its historic millstone, the restaurant combines timeless elegance, exceptional service and award-winning gastronomy.

For more than three decades, Old Mill has attracted loyal guests who return year after year to enjoy its distinctive cuisine and carefully curated wine selections.
