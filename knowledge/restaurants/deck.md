## Overview

Begin your day at Deck Restaurant while enjoying breathtaking views over Mirabello Bay.

Deck Restaurant serves as the main breakfast venue of Elounda Mare Hotel, offering both an extensive buffet breakfast and carefully prepared à la carte selections.

The relaxed atmosphere, elegant surroundings and stunning sea views create the perfect start to the day.
