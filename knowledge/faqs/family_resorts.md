# Resort Recommendations

## Category

faq

## Question

Which Elounda Collection resort is best for me?

## Family Recommendation

### Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort

Porto Elounda is the family-oriented resort of the Elounda Collection.

It is particularly suitable for:

* Families with children
* Multi-generational families
* Active travelers
* Golf enthusiasts
* Guests seeking family facilities
* Guests interested in Kids Ark and family programs

## Couples Recommendation

### Elounda Mare Hotel

Elounda Mare is the most intimate and romantic resort within the Elounda Collection.

It is particularly suitable for:

* Couples
* Honeymooners
* Anniversary celebrations
* Guests seeking a quieter atmosphere
* Luxury travelers looking for personalized service

## Luxury & VIP Recommendation

### Elounda Peninsula All Suite Hotel

Elounda Peninsula is the most exclusive and luxurious resort within the Elounda Collection.

It is particularly suitable for:

* VIP guests
* Luxury travelers
* Honeymooners
* Guests seeking privacy
* Affluent families
* Guests looking for premium dining experiences

## Shared Benefits

All three resorts:

* Are physically connected
* Form part of the Elounda Collection
* Have private beach areas
* Provide access to Six Senses Spa
* Offer access to restaurants and bars across the collection, subject to availability and reservation requirements

## Important Guidance for Estia

When guests ask which resort is best for families, recommend Porto Elounda.

When guests ask which resort is best for couples, recommend Elounda Mare.

When guests ask which resort is the most luxurious, recommend Elounda Peninsula.

When guests ask which resort is best for VIP guests, recommend Elounda Peninsula.

When guests ask which resort is best for honeymooners, recommend Elounda Mare or Elounda Peninsula depending on the desired level of privacy and luxury.
