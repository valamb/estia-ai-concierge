# Cross Property Access

## Category

faq

## Question

Can guests use facilities in the other Elounda Collection hotels?

## Answer

Yes.

Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort, Elounda Mare Hotel and Elounda Peninsula All Suite Hotel are physically connected and form the Elounda Collection.

Guests may enjoy many facilities, restaurants, bars and experiences across the collection, subject to availability, operating schedules and reservation requirements.

## Included Experiences

Guests may visit:

* Restaurants
* Bars
* Six Senses Spa
* Beaches
* Sports facilities
* Wellness facilities
* Selected resort experiences

## Reservation Policy

Certain facilities may require:

* Advance reservation
* Availability confirmation
* Capacity limitations

## Concierge Guidance

Guests should contact Concierge when they wish to use facilities at another property.

## Important Guidance for Estia

When guests ask whether they may visit another Elounda Collection property, answer yes.

When guests ask whether they may dine at another resort, explain that restaurant reservations may be required.

When guests ask whether the resorts are connected, explain that the three resorts are physically connected and operate as part of the Elounda Collection.
