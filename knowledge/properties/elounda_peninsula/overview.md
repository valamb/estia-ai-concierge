# Elounda Peninsula All Suite Hotel — Overview

## Property

Elounda Peninsula All Suite Hotel

## Category

property

## Overview

Elounda Peninsula All Suite Hotel is the most exclusive and luxurious resort within the Elounda Collection.

Situated on a private peninsula overlooking Mirabello Bay, the resort offers exceptional privacy, personalized service, spacious suites and villas, and a refined luxury experience.

Elounda Peninsula forms part of the Elounda Collection together with Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort and Elounda Mare Hotel.

Guests staying at Elounda Peninsula may enjoy facilities, restaurants and experiences throughout the Elounda Collection, subject to availability and reservation requirements.

## Resort Character

Elounda Peninsula is designed for guests seeking the highest level of luxury, privacy and personalized service.

It is particularly suitable for:

* Luxury couples
* VIP guests
* Honeymooners
* Affluent families
* Long-stay luxury travelers
* Guests seeking privacy and exclusivity

The resort offers a more exclusive and private atmosphere than Porto Elounda and Elounda Mare.

## Restaurants

### Calypso Restaurant

Elegant breakfast restaurant with spectacular views over Mirabello Bay.

Breakfast is served daily from 07:30 to 10:30.

### Il Borro Tuscan Bistro

Waterfront Italian restaurant inspired by the world-famous Tuscan estate owned by the Ferragamo family.

The restaurant celebrates authentic Italian cuisine, organic products and premium wines.

### Sumosan Restaurant

Internationally acclaimed contemporary Japanese restaurant.

Sumosan offers sushi, sashimi, Japanese specialties and signature cocktails in a unique waterfront setting overlooking the Aegean Sea.
