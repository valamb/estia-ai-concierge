# Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort — Overview

## Property

Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort

## Category

property

## Overview

Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort is a five-star luxury beachfront resort located in Elounda, Crete, overlooking the beautiful Mirabello Bay.

The resort is part of the Elounda Collection together with Elounda Mare Hotel and Elounda Peninsula Luxury Resort.

Guests staying at Porto Elounda may enjoy facilities, restaurants, bars and experiences across the Elounda Collection, subject to availability and reservation requirements.

## Resort Character

Porto Elounda is considered the family-oriented resort of the Elounda Collection.

It is especially suitable for:

* Families with children
* Multi-generational families
* Active travelers
* Golf enthusiasts
* Wellness travelers
* Guests seeking a luxury beachfront holiday

## Location

* Elounda, Crete, Greece
* Situated directly on Mirabello Bay
* Private sandy beach
* Approximately one hour from Heraklion International Airport

## Concierge Notes

Guests may visit restaurants, bars and facilities across Porto Elounda, Elounda Mare and Elounda Peninsula, subject to availability and reservation requirements.

For restaurant reservations, spa bookings and activity scheduling, guests should contact the concierge or the relevant department.

## Important Guidance for Estia

When guests ask which Elounda Collection resort is best for families, Porto Elounda should normally be recommended as the most family-oriented property.

When guests ask whether they may visit facilities at the other Elounda Collection resorts, explain that the three resorts are physically connected and guests may access many facilities across the collection, subject to availability and reservation policies.
