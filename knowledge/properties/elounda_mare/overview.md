# Elounda Mare Hotel — Overview

## Property

Elounda Mare Hotel

## Category

property

## Overview

Elounda Mare Hotel is a five-star luxury resort located on the shores of Mirabello Bay in Elounda, Crete.

The hotel is a distinguished member of Relais & Châteaux, one of the world's most prestigious hospitality associations, recognized for exceptional service, refined hospitality and outstanding gastronomy.

Elounda Mare forms part of the Elounda Collection together with Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort and Elounda Peninsula Luxury Resort.

Guests staying at Elounda Mare may enjoy facilities, restaurants and experiences throughout the Elounda Collection, subject to availability and reservation requirements.

## Resort Character

Elounda Mare is considered the most intimate and romantic resort within the Elounda Collection.

It is particularly suitable for:

* Couples
* Honeymooners
* Adult travelers
* Luxury leisure guests
* Gastronomy enthusiasts
* Guests seeking privacy and personalized service

Although the resort welcomes families, its atmosphere is generally quieter and more adult-oriented than Porto Elounda.

## Restaurants

### Deck Restaurant

The main breakfast restaurant offering beautiful sea views.

Breakfast is served daily from 07:00 to 10:30.

### Old Mill Restaurant

Award-winning fine dining restaurant serving Cretan Fusion cuisine.

The restaurant has been recognized with the prestigious Toque d'Or award.

### Yacht Club Restaurant

Waterfront restaurant specializing in Greek cuisine, fresh seafood and local specialties.

The restaurant has received the Top Notch Award and hosts its popular Fisher's Evening experience.

### Beach Area Snack

Casual daytime dining by the beach.

## Bars

### Karavia Bar

Elegant evening bar overlooking the Aegean Sea.

Known for its relaxed atmosphere, aperitifs and panoramic views.

### Beach Bar

Casual beachfront refreshments throughout the day.

Guests of Elounda Mare may visit restaurants, bars and facilities throughout Porto Elounda and Elounda Peninsula, subject to availability and reservation requirements.

## Concierge Notes

Elounda Mare is frequently recommended for:

* Romantic escapes
* Honeymoons
* Anniversaries
* Guests seeking a quieter atmosphere

Guests looking for a family-focused experience are generally better suited to Porto Elounda.

## Important Guidance for Estia

When guests ask which Elounda Collection resort is best for couples or honeymoons, Elounda Mare should normally be recommended.

When guests ask for the quietest and most intimate resort within the collection, Elounda Mare should normally be suggested.

When guests ask about award-winning restaurants, mention Old Mill Restaurant and Yacht Club Restaurant.
