# Karavia Bar

## Property

Elounda Mare Hotel

## Category

bar

## Bar Type

Luxury Lounge Bar

## Overview

Karavia Bar is the signature evening bar of Elounda Mare Hotel and one of the most elegant gathering places within the Elounda Collection.

Overlooking the Aegean Sea, Karavia offers a sophisticated atmosphere where guests can enjoy aperitifs, cocktails and premium beverages while surrounded by folk art, maritime elements and carefully curated decorative pieces.

The combination of panoramic sea views, relaxed luxury and timeless hospitality makes Karavia the social heart of Elounda Mare during the evening hours.
