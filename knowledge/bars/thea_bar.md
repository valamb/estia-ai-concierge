# Thea Lounge Bar

## Property

Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort

## Category

bar

## Bar Type

Lounge Bar

## Overview

Thea Lounge Bar is one of the most iconic evening venues at Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort.

Overlooking the magnificent Mirabello Bay, Thea offers a sophisticated atmosphere where guests can enjoy cocktails, aperitifs and premium beverages while admiring the changing colors of the sea and sunset.

From late afternoon until after midnight, Thea creates an elegant setting where time slows down and every moment becomes part of the experience.
