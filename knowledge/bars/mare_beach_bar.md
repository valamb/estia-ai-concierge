# Beach Bar Lounge

## Property

bar

## Overview

Adjacent to the private beach of Elounda Mare Hotel, Beach Bar Lounge serves as a relaxed gathering point where guests can enjoy refreshments, cocktails and light snacks throughout the day.

With its beachfront setting and laid-back atmosphere, the venue provides an ideal location for guests wishing to enjoy the sea, sunshine and hospitality of Elounda Mare.

Throughout the day, guests can relax with beverages and snacks while enjoying carefully selected music and views of Mirabello Bay.
