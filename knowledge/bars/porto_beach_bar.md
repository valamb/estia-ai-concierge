# Beach Bar & Lounge

## Property

Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort

## Category

bar

## Bar Type

Beach Bar & Lounge

## Overview

Located beside the private beach and seaside pool of Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort, Beach Bar & Lounge is one of the most popular daytime gathering points within the resort.

Throughout the day, guests can enjoy refreshing drinks, cocktails, coffees and light snacks beneath its distinctive wooden canopy while listening to carefully selected music and enjoying views of the sea.

As the day transitions into evening, the venue transforms into a relaxed lounge atmosphere where guests can continue enjoying premium drinks while overlooking Mirabello Bay.
