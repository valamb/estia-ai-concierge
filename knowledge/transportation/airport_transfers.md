# Airport Transfers

## Property

Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort
Elounda Mare Hotel
Elounda Peninsula All Suite Hotel

## Category

transportation

## Overview

Private airport transfer services are available between Heraklion International Airport (HER) and all Elounda Collection properties.

Transfers are designed to provide a seamless, comfortable and reliable experience for arrivals and departures throughout the season.

Advance reservations are strongly recommended.

## Primary Airport

### Heraklion International Airport (HER)

The primary airport serving all Elounda Collection properties.

Approximate transfer duration:

* 60 to 75 minutes
* Depending on traffic conditions

## Available Transfer Services

Guests may arrange:

* Airport Arrivals
* Airport Departures
* Port Transfers
* Private Chauffeur Services
* Local Transportation
* Luxury Transportation
* Excursions throughout Crete

## Vehicle Types

Available vehicle categories may include:

* Executive Sedan
* Luxury Sedan
* Minivan
* Luxury Minivan
* Mini Bus
* Coach

Vehicle allocation depends on:

* Number of passengers
* Number of suitcases
* Golf bags
* Special equipment
* Child seats

## Child Services

Child seats and baby seats may be available upon request.

Guests should request these at the time of booking.

## Information Required For Booking

To arrange a transfer, guests should provide:

* Flight number
* Arrival or departure date
* Arrival or departure time
* Number of passengers
* Number of suitcases
* Child seat requirements
* Hotel name

## Excursions

Private transportation may also be arranged for:

* Knossos
* Heraklion
* Lassithi Plateau
* Chania
* Rethymno
* Agios Nikolaos
* Other destinations throughout Crete

## Reservations

Advance booking is recommended.

Transportation requests should be arranged through Concierge or the Transportation Department.

## Concierge Notes

Vehicle selection should always take into account passenger count and luggage requirements.

Guests travelling with large luggage, golf bags or sports equipment may require larger vehicle categories.

## Important Guidance for Estia

When guests ask about airport transfers, explain that private transfer services are available between Heraklion Airport and all Elounda Collection properties.

When guests ask how long the transfer takes, answer approximately 60 to 75 minutes.

When guests ask what information is required for booking, explain that flight details, passenger count and luggage information are needed.

When guests ask about child seats, explain that child seats may be available upon request and should be requested when booking.
