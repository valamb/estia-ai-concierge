## Overview

Elounda Collection offers a range of premium concierge and VIP services designed to provide a seamless, highly personalised and luxury guest experience.

Our Concierge Team can assist guests before arrival, during their stay and after departure, ensuring every aspect of the journey is handled with discretion, efficiency and attention to detail.

Dedicated concierge assistance may include:

* Restaurant reservations
* Special occasion planning
* Anniversary celebrations
* Honeymoon arrangements
* Activity recommendations
* Cultural experiences
* Private excursions
* Transportation coordination

The objective of VIP Services is not only to fulfil requests but to anticipate guest preferences and create memorable experiences.

Many experiences can be customised according to guest preferences.

## Important Guidance for Estia

When guests ask about VIP services, explain that Elounda Collection offers personalised concierge assistance and premium guest experiences.

When guests ask about butler services, explain that butler services may be available for selected suites and villas.

When guests ask about airport VIP services, explain that Concierge may assist with fast-track, meet-and-greet and lounge arrangements subject to availability.

When guests ask for unique experiences, recommend personalised concierge planning through the Concierge Team.

When guests ask for luxury transportation, explain that executive chauffeur and premium transfer services may be arranged.
