## Overview

Elounda Collection offers bespoke yacht charter and private maritime experiences designed for guests seeking privacy, exclusivity and personalised luxury at sea.

Whether exploring secluded bays, discovering historic islands or enjoying a sunset cruise across the Aegean Sea, each experience can be tailored according to guest preferences.
