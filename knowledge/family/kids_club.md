# Kids Ark & Family Services

## Property

Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort

## Category

service

## Service Type

Family Services

## Overview

Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort is the family-oriented resort of the Elounda Collection and offers dedicated facilities and services for children and families.

The Kids Ark program is designed to provide a safe, engaging and enjoyable environment where children can participate in supervised activities while parents enjoy the resort's facilities.

## Family Facilities

Available family services may include:

* Kids Ark
* Children's activities
* Family experiences
* Creative programs
* Indoor and outdoor activities
* Babysitting services
* Nanny services

## Suitable For

* Families with children
* Young children
* School-age children
* Multi-generational families

## Activities

Activities may vary by season and age group.

Programs may include:

* Arts and crafts
* Games
* Educational activities
* Sports activities
* Outdoor experiences

## Babysitting

Babysitting and childcare services may be available upon request.

Advance booking is recommended.

## Reservations

Advance reservations may be required for selected family services and childcare programs.

## Recommended For

Kids Ark is recommended for:

* Families staying at Porto Elounda
* Parents seeking supervised children's activities
* Guests travelling with young children

## Location

Porto Elounda Golf & Spa Resort

## Concierge Notes

Kids Ark is one of the key family-oriented services of Porto Elounda.

Guests travelling with children should be informed about available family activities and childcare options.

## Important Guidance for Estia

When guests ask which resort is best for families, Porto Elounda should normally be recommended.

When guests ask about children's activities, mention Kids Ark and family programs.

When guests ask about babysitting services, explain that availability should be confirmed through Concierge.

When guests ask about childcare services, recommend contacting Concierge for current programs and schedules.
